Webinar | Nov 2 | Your API Strategy Controls Your Digital Transformation Journey

Mike Hacker October 21, 2021 Events

Tuesday, November 2, 2021 | 11:30 AM - 01:00 PM | (GMT-06:00) Central Time (US & Canada) - This is a joint event between Microsoft and Polaris Solutions.

Leverage the Azure platform to unleash your data

How do you harness your data to provide insights, enable digital experiences, take care of employees and customers and differentiate your business?  

Join Microsoft and our partner, Polaris Solutions, as you learn how to leverage APIs and Azure technologies to master your data management, while empowering your company with the tools to develop a clear data strategy and accelerate your digital success. 

In this first session, Microsoft & Polaris will take attendees on a journey through data. We will tap into APIs and Azure technologies and talk through the pieces needed to be successful in data management.
