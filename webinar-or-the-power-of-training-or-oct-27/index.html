Webinar | The Power of Training | Oct 27

October 27, 2:00 pm - 3:00 pm EDT presented by atmosera

Are your top technical performers potentially hurting your organization without you realizing it? Do you ask yourself if you should hire top new talent or invest in current team members? Are you losing talent to competitors causing a backlog of projects and a standstill on innovation?

If any of these questions resonate with you, it might be time to consider the power of training. Join our webinar on October 27th outlining the power training has had for organizations and explore what steps you can take to put the power back in your hands. We will look at concrete examples and trending data to help you make sense of the value training will bring to your organization. Anyone who helps oversee technical teams within their organization should attend this eye-opening webinar.
