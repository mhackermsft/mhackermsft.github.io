Webinar | Apr 20 | Moving Beyond Lift and Shift

Mike Hacker April 8, 2022 Events

Hosted by CAST Software | April 20, 1PM CST

Moving beyond lift & shift to modernize critical applications is a high-stakes, high-anxiety affair for IT leaders, especially when looking at refactoring and shifting them to cloud. Lack of accurate knowledge and insight into the inner working of the applications further exacerbates the challenge during the migration process.

CAST Software, a global leader in Software Intelligence and Application Modernization, will share how customers are refactoring and migrating applications to cloud with a smarter, transparent and more automated approach. Enterprises globally are using automated application insights from CAST to modernize legacy business critical applications with control, speed and accuracy leveraging cloud native micro services saving months of time and avoiding u-turns common to manual approach.

In this session, CAST will share the approach and real-world examples.

Learn about:

  • Key challenges to be aware of when moving to cloud native and containers
  • Benefits of refactoring apps for PaaS vs rehosting to IaaS
  • How to use software intelligence to de-risk and accelerate the transformation
