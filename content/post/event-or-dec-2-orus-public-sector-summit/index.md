*8:00am - 9:30am PT / 11am - 12:30pm ET*

Microsoft is hosting a US Public Sector Summit on Thursday, December 2nd for government and education decision makers.  This digital experience is focused on the future and celebrating the public sector's resilience, agility, and achievements over the course of the pandemic.  The event will feature

-   Public sector organizations driving mission impact, data-driven excellence, and research and healthcare innovation. Top leaders from the US Navy, Choctaw Nation of Oklahoma, State of Illinois Department of Children and Family Services, Nebraska Department of Education, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and more will share their real-world success stories.
-   Insights from thought leaders, including Microsoft’s Satya Nadella, Brad Smith, Dr. Julia Glidden, and others on the future of infrastructure and cybersecurity, workforce skills, sustainability, and additional public sector priorities.
