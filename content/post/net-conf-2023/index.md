November 14 - 16, 2023 | Online | Free

.NET Conf is an annual online conference that showcases the latest developments and innovations in the .NET platform, open source, and dev tools. It is a free and interactive event that features speakers from the community and the .NET team, live sessions, Q&A, trivia games, and prizes. It is a great opportunity for .NET developers to learn new skills, network with peers, and have fun. This year, .NET Conf 2023 will take place on November 14-16 2023. 

[Learn more](https://www.dotnetconf.net/)
