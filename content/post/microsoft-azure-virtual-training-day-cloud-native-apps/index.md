**Microsoft Azure Virtual Training Day: Cloud-Native Apps**  
*Tuesday, July 26, 2022 | 9:00 AM - 11:45 AM | (GMT-08:00) Pacific Time (US & Canada)*  
*Wednesday, July 27, 2022 | 9:00 AM - 11:45 AM | (GMT-08:00) Pacific Time (US & Canada)*

Join us at Microsoft Azure Virtual Training Day: Cloud-Native Apps to learn best practices for creating the foundation of your cloud-based environment. You’ll gain knowledge about selecting the best components for your apps, putting those components together, and deploying the completed apps to Azure. Discover the storage, security, and performance features that will help your web, mobile, and analytics apps thrive in the cloud.
