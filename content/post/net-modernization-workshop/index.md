**Microsoft Azure Immersion Workshop: .NET App Modernization**  
*Friday, August 26, 2022 | 11:00 AM - 3:30 PM | (GMT-05:00) Eastern Time (US & Canada)*

Join Microsoft and a specialized partner to explore new and improved ways to manage and create efficiencies in your .NET application estate at Microsoft Azure Immersion Workshop: .NET App Modernization. You’ll learn how to evaluate and prioritize your .NET apps for modernization—while limiting interruptions, increasing resiliency and scalability, and preparing for migration and hosting on Azure.
