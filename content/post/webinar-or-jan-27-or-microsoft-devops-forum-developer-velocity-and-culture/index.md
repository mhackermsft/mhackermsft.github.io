*Thursday, January 27, 2022 09:00 AM – 1:00 PM | (GMT-08:00) Pacific Time (US & Canada) - Hosted by Microsoft and Insight.*

Join us for an intimate virtual forum and learn how to measure and improve your company’s Developer Velocity from the experts at Microsoft, GitHub, and \[Insight\]. What’s in store during the event:

1.  Learn about Developer Velocity and the free tools available to increase developer productivity.
2.  A breakdown of the advantages derived from adopting new ways of measuring Developer Velocity and the pitfalls to avoid.
3.  The cultural transformation required to get there—and the opportunities that arise from such a shift.
4.  Perspectives from Microsoft DevOps specialists, GitHub, \[Insight\], and other industry-leading enterprises.

Even if your organization is in the early stages of your Developer Velocity journey, you can still learn how to unlock your company’s full potential. In an effort to keep discussions intimate and engaging, seats will be limited, [so RSVP now to reserve yours](https://mktoevents.com/Microsoft+Event/315228/157-GQE-382)!
