Webinar | Nov 16 | The Power of GitOps for Deploying Frequently

Mike Hacker November 3, 2021 Events

 

Nov 16, 2021 | 2:00 pm - 3:30 pm Eastern Time
 
Teams across organizations keep learning about "Kubernetes" and deciding to try it for greenfield/brownfield projects, especially with microservices-style architectures. Commonly, many of these teams continue to use basic push-based CI/CD practices that they would generally use on other platforms. However, Kubernetes has a massive advantage over other platforms, and that's GitOps.
 
Learn about the power of GitOps for Kubernetes-based workload so that you can understand how application teams can be more efficient and deliver more value to the business.
 
We'll cover:
  • The typical CI/CD pipeline
  • The problems, especially with microservices
  • What is GitOps?
  • Understanding its power
  • When it's not a great fit
  • Choosing a solution, quick comparison
    • ArgoCD
    • Azure Arc (Flux)
    • Tekton
