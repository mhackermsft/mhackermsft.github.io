Microsoft Build 2024

Mike Hacker April 24, 2024 Events

The Microsoft Build Conference 2024 is scheduled to take place from May 21 to May 23, 2024. This year, the conference offers a hybrid experience, allowing attendees to participate either in-person at Seattle’s new Summit Building or online. Registration is open, priced at $2,125 for the in-person event or free for the digital event. Participants can learn from industry experts, engage in hands-on sessions with the latest AI innovations, and connect with the vibrant developer community.

Check out the Microsoft Build Website for more details and to register.
