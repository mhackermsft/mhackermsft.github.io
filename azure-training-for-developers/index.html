Azure Training for Developers

Mike Hacker August 26, 2022 Training

Below you will find a collection of self-paced Microsoft Learn learning paths and modules which will help Developers get up to speed on Microsoft Azure.  By completing the fundamentals modules you will be preparing for the AZ-900 certification.  If you complete the AZ-204 modules you are well on your way to passing the Microsoft Developer Associates certification.  I have also included some content on Java, Infrastructure as code, and some general development best practices.
