Webinar | Oct 21 | Optimizing Compute on Azure with Azure Kubernetes Services

Azure Kubernetes Services is not a one-size-fits-all tool. An organization can tailor AKS to meet its goals in several ways, ranging from single-node development clusters to dynamically scaling, high-performance clusters for production workloads. In this webinar, we will look at all the knobs and switches you can use to shape AKS to align with your app needs. We’ll cover autoscaling, node pools, and virtual nodes to give you the whole gamut of all the possibilities.
