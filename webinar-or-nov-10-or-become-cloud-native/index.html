Webinar | Nov 10 | Become Cloud Native

Mike Hacker November 5, 2021 Events

Wednesday, November 10, 2021 | 9:00AM – 10AM PST | Joint event with Nous Infosystems

As enterprises are looking to digitally transform, modernizing their applications to become cloud-native is on top of their agenda.

Join us and our partner, Nous Infosystems to learn how to build agile, scalable, and cost-efficient cloud-native applications or modernize existing ones using Azure's fully managed services such as Azure API management, Azure Kubernetes Service, and Azure Functions to seamlessly integrate microservices based application workloads to perform complex functions and automate business critical workloads across the organization.

In this webinar, you will learn:

  • What is cloud-native and why it’s important
  • Approaches to building cloud-native apps
  • Modernization Case studies
  • Nous application modernization offer

Learn more and register here.
